## 1. Positioning Shift

### Current State
- Generic headline: "Solusi Digital untuk Praktik Kesehatan Anda"
- Two plans shown equally (Template marked "coming soon", Custom marked "most popular")
- Copy doesn't emphasize affordability or "done-for-you" angle
- Messaging focused on professionalism, not pain-point removal

### New State
- Lead with affordability + pain removal: "Website Profesional Hanya 20 Ribu/Bulan — Tanpa Repot Server & Domain"
- Template becomes PRIMARY plan (marked "Recommended"), Custom becomes secondary
- Copy emphasizes speed, simplicity, and all-included pricing
- Target audience: busy nakes who want it fast and cheap, not tech-savvy builders
